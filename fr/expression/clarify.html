Asking someone to repeat — and saying you didn't understand

The flip side of Understanding: what to say when speech flies past you. These are the phrases that keep a real conversation alive — buy yourself a repeat, slow the other person down, and check you got it right. Grouped by what you need:

Pardon ? / Comment ?  (the polite “sorry, what?”)

"Pardon ?"

“Sorry?” — the safest, most common choice.


"Comment ?"

“What was that?” — neutral and polite.


"Quoi ? / Hein ?"

“What? / Huh?” — informal and can sound blunt.


⚠️ With people you don't know, use Pardon ? or Comment ? — Quoi ? and Hein ? come across as abrupt.



Tu peux répéter ?  (say it again, please)

"Tu peux répéter, s'il te plaît ?"

“Can you say that again, please?”


"Vous pouvez répéter ?"

“Could you repeat that?” — vous.


Plus lentement  (the learner's lifeline)

"Tu peux parler moins vite ?"

“Can you speak more slowly?”


"Plus lentement, s'il te plaît."

“More slowly, please.”


"Tu peux parler plus fort ?"

“Can you speak up?”



J'ai pas compris  (from “not quite” to “totally lost”)

"J'ai pas (bien) compris."

“I didn't (quite) understand.”


"Je comprends pas."

“I don't understand.”


"J'ai pas tout saisi."

“I didn't catch all of it.”


"Je suis un peu perdu(e)."

“I'm a bit lost.”



Qu'est-ce que tu veux dire ?  (asking for the meaning)

"Qu'est-ce que tu veux dire par là ?"

“What do you mean by that?”


"C'est-à-dire ?"

“Meaning? / How so?” — short and very natural.


"Dans quel sens ?"

“In what sense?”


"Tu peux préciser ?"

“Can you be more specific?”



Comment on dit… ?  (reaching for a word)

"Comment on dit ‘X' en français ?"

“How do you say ‘X' in French?”


"Ça veut dire quoi, ‘X' ?"

“What does ‘X' mean?”


"Ça s'écrit comment ?"

“How's it spelled?”


"C'est quoi le mot pour… ?"

“What's the word for…?”



Si j'ai bien compris…  (reflecting it back)

"Si j'ai bien compris, tu veux dire que… ?"

“If I understood correctly, you mean that…?”


"Donc, en gros… ?"

“So, basically…?”


"C'est bien ça ?"

“Is that right?”


"Attends, tu dis que… ?"

“Wait, you're saying that…?”



À retenir

Keep Pardon ? ready as your reflex — polite and universal. For a real repeat, tu peux répéter, moins vite ? does double duty. And the single most useful habit for comprehension is reflecting it back: Si j'ai bien compris, … c'est bien ça ?
